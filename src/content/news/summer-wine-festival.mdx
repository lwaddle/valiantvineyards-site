## An Evening Under the Stars

We're thrilled to announce our annual Summer Wine Festival! Join us at Buffalo Run Resort for an unforgettable evening celebrating South Dakota's finest wines and spirits.

This year's festival will feature selections from our award-winning wine collection alongside small-batch spirits from Stone Faces Distillery. Whether you're a fan of our signature Valiant red or prefer to explore our newest white varietals, there's something for every palate.

## What to Expect

- **Wine Tastings** - Sample our full selection of wines, including limited releases
- **Live Music** - Local musicians performing throughout the evening
- **Food Trucks** - Delicious pairings from regional favorites
- **Distillery Tours** - Go behind the scenes at Stone Faces Distillery
- **Missouri River Views** - Watch the sunset from our terrace

## Event Details

The festival runs from 4:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Tickets include a commemorative wine glass and five tasting tokens. Additional tokens available for purchase.

We recommend arriving early to secure the best spots on the terrace. Seating is limited, but standing room is plentiful throughout our beautiful grounds.

**Designated driver discounts available.** Please drink responsibly.

We can't wait to raise a glass with you!
